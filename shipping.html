Shipping policy.

Where, when, and how much. We ship to all 50 US states from our California fulfilment partner.

Rates & transit time

Transit times begin the business day after your order ships, not the day you place it.

Processing time

Most orders leave our warehouse within 1–2 business days. During launches and holiday windows, processing can stretch to 3 business days — we'll email you the moment your package gets a tracking number.

Tracking

You'll get a tracking email from help@homeoften.com as soon as the carrier scans your label. If you don't see it after three business days, check spam, then reach out — we'll resend it.

Where we ship

  • All 50 US states, plus DC.
  • APO/FPO/DPO military addresses (transit can take an extra 5–10 business days).
  • US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, USVI): standard rate applies, transit 7–14 business days.
  • International: not at the moment. Sign up for the newsletter and we'll announce when this opens.

P.O. boxes & addresses we can't deliver to

Most items ship via USPS and reach P.O. boxes without issue. Larger appliances (air fryers, robot vacuums, dispensers) ship via UPS or FedEx and need a physical street address — at checkout we'll flag any items that can't go to a P.O. box.

Damaged or missing on arrival

Inspect your order on delivery. If a box arrives crushed or an item is broken, photograph the package and the item, then email help@homeoften.com within 7 days. We replace or refund damaged items — no return required for visible shipping damage.

Lost packages

If tracking shows your package as delivered but you can't find it, give it 24 hours (carriers sometimes mark "delivered" before the truck actually arrives). After 24 hours, contact us and we'll open a carrier trace and ship a replacement.

Address changes: we can change a shipping address up until the package leaves the warehouse (typically 12–24 hours after order). After that, the carrier handles redirects — call 888-123-1234 as fast as you can and we'll help.

Questions?

Call 888-123-1234 or email help@homeoften.com, Mon–Fri 9am–6pm PT.
